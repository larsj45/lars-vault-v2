# Market Validation Playbook

## Goal

Validate demand before heavy investment.

## Validation Framework

### Step 1 — Hypothesis

Define:
- target customer
- core pain point
- proposed solution
- expected value

### Step 2 — Offer

Create:
- landing page
- positioning
- lead capture
- simple CTA

### Step 3 — Traffic

Possible channels:
- LinkedIn
- outbound outreach
- partnerships
- SEO
- communities
- referrals

### Step 4 — Signal Evaluation

Measure:
- inbound interest
- lead quality
- conversion rates
- customer conversations
- willingness to pay

### Step 5 — Decision

Outcomes:
- continue
- iterate
- pivot
- archive

## Principle

Fast feedback is more valuable than extensive planning.
