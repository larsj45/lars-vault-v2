# Decision Logs

Document important strategic and operational decisions.

## Purpose

Decision logs help:

- preserve context
- reduce repeated debates
- improve continuity
- support AI agents
- compound operational knowledge

## Recommended Format

```md
# YYYY-MM-DD

## Decision

What was decided?

## Why

Why was the decision made?

## Alternatives Considered

What alternatives were evaluated?

## Risks

What risks exist?

## Follow-Up

What should happen next?
```

## Principle

Important decisions should become durable operational memory.
